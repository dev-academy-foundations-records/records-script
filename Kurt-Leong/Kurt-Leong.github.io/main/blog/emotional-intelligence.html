  1. What is Emotional Intelligence?

    2. Emotional Intelligence abbreviate as EI and is also know as Emotional Quotient (EQ), which is used to describe the ability of individuls to recognise, interpret, understand, and manage different types of emotions. This ability allows people to effectively communicate with each other, to relieve the stress, emphathize with other and deal with challenges and conflics. By achieve this, individuals can build strong relationship with other, and play a better social role.

    There are four commonly defined attributes of EQ:

    • Self Management

      • It means one is able to regulate strong emotions that may cause impulsive behaviours. It also means the individuals can think the consequences before taking actions

    • Self Awareness

      • If someone are self-aware, it means he or she can see own patterns of behaviours and motives. Individuals can name their emotions, and they have the ability to explore where are the emotions coming from, and why the emotions come this way. They can also identify their strength, and know their limitations.

    • Socil Awareness

      • This means one have empathy, individuals can understand others' emotion, needs and concerns by recongising emotional cues.They feel comfortable and confident in various of social situations

    • Relationship Management

      • This aspeact describes people can develop and mantain good relationships with others. They can also communicate clearly, inspire and influence others.

  2. How is EQ different to IQ?

    3. IQ usually reffers to one's intelectual ability such as use logic to solve problems, learn abstract concepts, adapt changes or use language. While EQ reffers to the ability to sense and manage emotions of oneself and others. Both IQ and EQ are importants to individuals, people do not think with one part of brain in daily life.

    4. The reason Why EQ is crucial is because that humanbings are social animals, as individual, we are firmly connected with others. In this social world, one with sound Emotional intelligence is capable to deal with negative feelings, conflics in daily life.
