Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (EQ) refers to the ability to recognize, understand, and manage one's own emotions, as well as the emotions of others.

How is EQ different to IQ?

Intelligence quotient (IQ) is a measure of cognitive ability, or a person's ability to think and reason. It typically measures things like problem-solving, abstract reasoning, and memory.
While IQ and EQ are related, they are not the same thing and measure different aspects of intelligence. A person can have a high IQ and a low EQ, or vice versa.

Why is EQ important?

EQ has been shown to be important for success in personal and professional settings, and is associated with better mental health and well-being.
