Bootcamp Learning Plan

1. What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I am hoping to use the skills I have gained during this course to make web applications and contribute to the success of the employer.

2. What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My strengths would be still be curiosity, perseverance, and problem-solving skills. However, my limitations that I think of in boootcamp would be different from the ones for foundations. I lack the experience of working with others, so communication and collaboration within the team might be the limitations for me. As I have foreseen my weaknesses in bootcamp, I would prepare myself with a humble attitude and seek help from facilitators when necessary.

3. What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

The biggest challenge would be working in groups and speaking up in a group. As I said before, I have no experience of programming within a team. Communication and collaboration would be the biggest challenges for me.

4. What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

communication, teamwork within groups, pairing with others, giving and receiving feedback, developing some leadership skills.

5. What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I believe that the bootcamp team would do their best to support all the students. For me, I just want the team to be reachable when needed (not 7/24 of course).

6. What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I hope myself to keep focused throughout the programme and try my best to complete single tasks, pairing tasks and group tasks. I would seek help from facilitators when I feel stressed and confused about the learning goal and also update my learning progress with them regularly.
